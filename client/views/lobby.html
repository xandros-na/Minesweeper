How to Play

  1. Click the Join button to connect to the game queue. A game needs at least two people to Play!
  2. Once the game starts, your "mines" will be coloured Red and your opponent's will be coloured Blue
  3. Each player has 15 seconds per turn. If you get a mine, the timer resets and you get to go again!
  4. There are 30 hidden mines on the board. Collect more mines than your opponent!
